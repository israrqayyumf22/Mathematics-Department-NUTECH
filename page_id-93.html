1. University Open Day

Join us for the University Open Day on August 15th, where you can explore our campus and learn about our programs.

Semester registration for the Fall 2024 term is now open. Please register by August 25th to secure your classes.

Our new research lab will be officially opened on September 1st. All students and faculty are invited to attend the opening ceremony.

We are hosting a series of guest lectures from leading industry experts starting September 10th. Check the events calendar for details.

A career counseling session will be held on September 20th for all students seeking career advice and internship opportunities.

The Annual Science Fair will take place on October 5th. Students are encouraged to showcase their projects and innovations.

Check out the new course offerings for the upcoming semester. Details are available on the course registration portal.

Health and Wellness Week will run from January 10th to January 15th. Various activities and workshops will be organized to promote student well-being.

Applications for scholarships are open until February 1st. Visit the financial aid office for more details on eligibility and application procedures.

14. Alumni Networking Event

An alumni networking event will be held on February 20th. Connect with former students and expand your professional network.

15. Summer Internship Program

The Summer Internship Program applications are open from March 1st to March 31st. Apply through the career services office.

16. Research Conference

Our annual research conference will take place on April 10th. Present your research and attend sessions led by prominent researchers.

The student council elections will be held on April 25th. Voting will be conducted online, and results will be announced on April 30th.

Various summer camps will be organized for students from May 5th to May 25th. Register early to secure your spot.

A campus safety seminar will be conducted on June 1st. All students are encouraged to attend to learn about safety protocols.

20. End of Year Celebration

Join us for the End of Year Celebration on June 15th. There will be awards, performances, and a chance to celebrate the year's achievements.
