About Our Department

The Mathematics Department at National Univeristy of Technology, NUTECH is a vibrant and dynamic community of scholars, educators, and students.

Our department offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers a wide range of mathematical disciplines, including algebra, calculus, differential equations, statistics, and applied mathematics.

Our mission is to foster a deep understanding of mathematical concepts and their applications, encourage critical thinking and problem-solving skills, and promote the discovery of new mathematical knowledge through research. We strive to create an inclusive and collaborative environment where all members can thrive and contribute to the advancement of mathematics.

Students in our department benefit from small class sizes, personalized instruction, and numerous opportunities for research and professional development. We also provide various extracurricular activities, including math clubs, seminars, and competitions, to enhance the learning experience and build a strong sense of community.
Whether you are a prospective student, current student, or alumni, we are here to support your academic and professional aspirations. We look forward to welcoming you to the Mathematics Department at National Univeristy of Technology, NUTECH.
