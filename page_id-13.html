Upcoming Events at the Mathematics Department

Mathematics Conference 2024

Event Description:

The Mathematics Conference 2024 is an annual gathering that attracts leading mathematicians, researchers, and students from around the world. The conference aims to explore recent advancements in mathematics and foster collaborations across various disciplines. Keynote speeches, panel discussions, and paper presentations will be featured over the three-day event.

Chief Guests:

  • Dr. Ahmed Khan - Renowned Mathematician from MIT
  • Dr. Fatima Ali - Distinguished Professor at LUMS
  • Prof. Maria Hassan - Leading Researcher at Cambridge University

Event Duration:

The conference will be held from September 12th to September 14th, 2024. The first day includes keynote speeches, the second day features panel discussions and workshops, and the third day concludes with paper presentations and networking sessions.

Venue:

Main Auditorium, Mathematics Department, NUTECH Campus.

Schedule:

Date Time Activity
September 12 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM Keynote Speeches
September 12 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM Panel Discussions
September 13 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM Workshops
September 13 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM Research Presentations
September 14 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM Networking and Closing Remarks

Additional Information:

For registration and further details, please visit the official conference website or contact the organizers at events@mathsdept.nutech.edu.pk.

Annual Mathematics Quiz Competition

Event Description:

The Annual Mathematics Quiz Competition aims to challenge students' mathematical knowledge and problem-solving skills. The competition is open to high school and undergraduate students, featuring individual and team events. Prizes and certificates will be awarded to the top performers.

Chief Guests:

  • Dr. Tariq Jameel - Expert in Mathematical Education
  • Ms. Sara Bukhari - Educational Consultant
  • Mr. Omar Shah - Director of Educational Programs

Event Duration:

The quiz competition will be held on October 5th, 2024, from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. The day includes preliminary rounds, semifinals, and the grand finale.

Venue:

University Conference Hall, NUTECH Campus.

Schedule:

Time Activity
10:00 AM - 11:30 AM Preliminary Rounds
11:30 AM - 12:30 PM Semifinals
1:00 PM - 2:00 PM Finals
2:00 PM - 3:00 PM Prize Distribution
3:00 PM - 4:00 PM Closing Ceremony

Additional Information:

Participants must register by September 25th, 2024. For registration and details, contact quiz@mathsdept.nutech.edu.pk.
